Welcome to our page

Established in 2012, Wilan Oil Field Services Ltd is an indigenous wholly owned Ghanaian Rope Access Company based in Accra. The company was established by a group of highly talented individuals, whose skills encapsulate essential areas of business management and rope access.

Our business offers practical rope access solutions to our clients with the intention to expand to international levels. We render such services by assembling highly qualified teams from our staff complement. Through synergies and diversity, we solve problems, which in turn unlock the most value for our clients.
