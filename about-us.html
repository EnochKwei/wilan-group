Who we are.

We are a trusted brand with so many years of experience.

Wilan Support Services Ltd

Established in 2012, Wilan Oil Field Services Ltd is an indigenous wholly owned Ghanaian Rope Access Company based in Accra. The company was established by a group of highly talented individuals, whose skills encapsulate essential areas of business management and rope access. Our business offers practical rope access solutions to our clients with the intention to expand to international levels. We render such services by assembling highly qualified teams from our staff complement. Through synergies and diversity, we solve problems, which in turn unlock the most value for our clients.

Our Values

Having had the collective experience in managing and running many Rope Access projects as well as successful business ventures we have gained the necessary skills and expertise required in order to deliver a holistic, whilst specialized service. Cooperation and strategic planning ensure that the client’s needs are met beyond their expectations. This sets us apart from our competitors. Wilan Oil Field Services aims to provide a cost effective, yet highly professional service.

Our Rate Card

Wilan derives its full project managing rates through on-site evaluation. These rates vary according to the project as well as resources required to perform the service. We also provide labour to clients at a daily rate, which would be determined by the skills and experience of the technician.
